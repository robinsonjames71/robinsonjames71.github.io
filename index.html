Welcome

Here at Worx Automotive we’ve been servicing and maintaining European vehicles since 1987. Our technicians are factory trained and have specialised in working on European cars. Our aim is to ensure every time you visit our 900sqm state-of-the art workshop, it’s the most enjoyable, personable service experience you’ve had.

We understand that every client is different and every car is different. That’s why we offer tailor made maintenance schedules to suit your requirements.

Have peace of mind, that when you walk into Worx, the person you speak to over the counter, is also the person that’s working on your vehicle. We believe in including you in all aspects of your vehicles service, and our personal touch will ensure you never feel left in the dark.

Loan cars are available on booking requests.

So, how do we compare to the rest?

Worx Automotive has built a reputation on providing its clients with nothing but the highest quality parts and accessories. Rest assured that when you bring your new vehicle to Worx, we guarantee it will maintain its new car warranty, because we only install genuine or OEM parts on your vehicle. This means that not only will your car leave with a warranty on the work completed, but also a warranty from the manufacturer on every part that was installed.

We’ve partnered with Liqui Moly, bringing you over 50 years of experience in lubricants and fuel & oil additives developed with Europe’s leading car manufactures. Liqui Moly formulations are ideal for prestige, performance, muscle and classic cars as well as 4WD’s and competition engines.

We enable our clients’ access to exclusive dealer support systems and provide the latest technology in diagnostic systems. We use all the same tools that you would find in a factory dealership.
