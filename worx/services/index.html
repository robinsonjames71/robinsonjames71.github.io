Vehicle Servicing

It's important to keep up with the scheduled maintenance requirements of your car to ensure long-term performance, efficiency, safety and peace of mind.
When servicing your vehicle with Worx Automotive, we complete a comprehensive safety check and advise of any areas that require immediate attention, or may cause issues in the future. This information will provide you with a clear image of the condition of your car, while we continue to ensure it's reliability between services.
With particular makes and models we can also recommend a suitable interim service plan, which helps reduce wear and tear on vital engine components, above and beyond the manufacturer's recommendations.
All services are undertaken with genuine or original equipment manufacturer parts and lubricants of equal or higher quality.
